Hello World!

So, this is it: the first post in the @MattjCamp blog. And like many things in the technology world, this post will start with the venerable "Hello World!" phrase.

Since this is the first post, I'm going to take a few moments to discuss why I'm setting this platform up. Here's the deal: I want to re-couple my online presence back to me. For so many years, everything I've done online have been for my company Mobile App Mastery.

But, Mobile App Mastery is in the process of unwinding. By the end of the year, the company will be either gone completely or replaced by a next generation lifestyle company

I still want a voice and so this blog will be it. I'll be talking a lot about technology here, but I'll also be doing other kinds of writing. So, keep an eye here to see what I'm up too!
