Matt Campbell

Hi, I'm Matt Campbell and this blog is where I write about online business, iPhone apps and technology.

Online Business

I love talking about online business, sometimes known as Internet or lifestyle business. These are these business run by one or two people, typically from home or while traveling, that exist entirely online.

From 2008 to 2014 I had my own online business called App Shop, also known as Mobile App Mastery. I lived the 4 Hour Work Week.

In 2014 I lost everything and was devestated. I write about it all here, including those gray and dark areas the gurus never mention.

iPhone Apps

In 2008, I was inspired by the App Store - I dropped everything to learn and eventually published iPhone apps. This was my gateway to online business.

Getting back into the iPhone app scene is a huge priority for me. I'll be writing about my experiences with Xcode and Swift as I do a major reboot of my iPhone app lineup.

Data Science

Data science is what I do professionally. The term data science refers to the practice of using technology along with analytical methods to gain insight.

I primarily do this using a programming language named R along with data visualization tools like Tableau.
